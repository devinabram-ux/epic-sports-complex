Who We Are

The Region's First Real Athletic Home

Southwest Mississippi and Southeast Louisiana has always produced elite athletes. For the first time, it has a facility to match them. EPIC Sports Complex is the only fully controlled, extended-hours, multi-sport training and competition center in the region.

No more driving two hours for a tournament. No more cancelled practices when it rains. No more letting talent walk out of the community for want of a place to grow.
